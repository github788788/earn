 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Item&nbsp;8.01. Other Events. </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On November&nbsp;2, 2017, Philip Morris International Inc. (&#147;PMI&#148;) issued US$750,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its 1.875% Notes due 2019 (the
&#147;2019 Notes&#148;), US$750,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its 2.500% Notes due 2022 (the &#147;2022 Notes&#148;) and US$500,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its 3.125% Notes due 2028 (the &#147;2028 Notes&#148; and, together with
the 2019 Notes and 2022 Notes, the &#147;Notes&#148;). The Notes were issued pursuant to an Indenture dated as of April&nbsp;25, 2008, by and between PMI and HSBC Bank USA, National Association, as trustee.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In connection with the issuance of the Notes, on October&nbsp;31, 2017, PMI entered into a Terms Agreement (the &#147;Terms Agreement&#148;) with Barclays
Capital Inc., Citigroup Global Markets Inc., Credit Suisee Securities (USA) LLC, Deutsche Bank Securities Inc., ING Financial Markets LLC and Santander Investment Securities Inc., as representatives of the several underwriters named therein (the
&#147;Underwriters&#148;), pursuant to which PMI agreed to issue and sell the Notes to the Underwriters. The provisions of an Underwriting Agreement, dated as of April&nbsp;25, 2008 (the &#147;Underwriting Agreement&#148;), are incorporated by
reference in the Terms Agreement.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">PMI has filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission a Prospectus dated February&nbsp;14,
2017 and a Prospectus Supplement (the &#147;Prospectus Supplement&#148;) dated October&nbsp;31, 2017 (Registration <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">No.&nbsp;333-216046)</FONT> in connection with the public offering of the Notes. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Notes are subject to certain customary covenants, including limitations on PMI&#146;s ability, with significant exceptions, to incur debt secured by liens
and engage in sale/leaseback transactions. PMI may redeem the Notes, in whole or in part, at the applicable redemption prices described in the Prospectus Supplement, plus accrued and unpaid interest thereon to, but excluding, the applicable
redemption date. PMI may also redeem all, but not part, of the Notes of each series upon the occurrence of specified tax events as described in the Prospectus Supplement. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Interest on the 2019 Notes is payable from November&nbsp;2, 2017 semiannually in arrears on May&nbsp;1 and November&nbsp;1, commencing May&nbsp;1, 2018, to
holders of record on the preceding April&nbsp;16 and October&nbsp;17, as the case may be. Interest on the 2022 Notes is payable from November&nbsp;2, 2017 semiannually in arrears on May&nbsp;2 and November&nbsp;2, commencing May&nbsp;2, 2018, to
holders of record on the preceding April&nbsp;17 and October&nbsp;18, as the case may be. Interest on the 2028 Notes is payable from November&nbsp;2, 2017 semiannually in arrears on March&nbsp;2 and September&nbsp;2, commencing March&nbsp;2, 2018,
to holders of record on the preceding February&nbsp;15 and August&nbsp;18, as the case may be. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The 2019 Notes will mature on November&nbsp;1, 2019, the
2022 Notes will mature on November&nbsp;2, 2022 and the 2028 Notes will mature on March&nbsp;2, 2028. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Notes will be PMI&#146;s senior unsecured
obligations and will rank equally in right of payment with all of its existing and future senior unsecured indebtedness. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">For a complete description of
the terms and conditions of the Underwriting Agreement, the Terms Agreement and the Notes, please refer to such agreements and the form of Notes, each of which is incorporated herein by reference and is an exhibit to this report as Exhibits 1.1,
1.2, 4.1, 4.2 and 4.3, respectively. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Certain of the Underwriters and their respective affiliates have, from time to time, performed, and may in the
future perform, various financial advisory, commercial and investment banking services for PMI, for which they received or will receive customary fees and expenses. Certain of the Underwriters and their respective affiliates are lenders under
PMI&#146;s credit facilities. In addition, certain of the Underwriters or their respective affiliates act as dealers in connection with PMI&#146;s commercial paper programs. </P>
