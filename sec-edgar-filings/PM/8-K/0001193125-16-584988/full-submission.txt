 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Item&nbsp;8.01. Other Events. </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">On May&nbsp;10, 2016, Philip Morris International Inc. (&#147;PMI&#148;) issued US$500,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its 2.125% Notes
due 2023 (the &#147;2023 Notes&#148;) and US$500,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its 4.250% Notes due 2044 (the &#147;2044 Notes&#148; and, together with the 2023 Notes, the &#147;Notes&#148;). The 2044 Notes are a further issuance of the
US$750,000,000 aggregate principal amount of 4.250% Notes due 2044 issued by PMI on November&nbsp;10, 2014. The Notes were issued pursuant to an Indenture (the &#147;Indenture&#148;) dated as of April&nbsp;25, 2008, by and between PMI and HSBC Bank
USA, National Association, as trustee (the &#147;Trustee&#148;). </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">In connection with the issuance of the Notes, on April&nbsp;28, 2016, PMI
entered into a Terms Agreement (the &#147;Terms Agreement&#148;) with Barclays Capital Inc., Citigroup Global Markets Inc., HSBC Securities (USA) Inc., J.P. Morgan Securities LLC, Santander Investment Securities Inc. and Soci&eacute;t&eacute;
G&eacute;n&eacute;rale, as representatives of the several underwriters named therein (the &#147;Underwriters&#148;), pursuant to which PMI agreed to issue and sell the Notes to the Underwriters. The provisions of an Underwriting Agreement, dated as
of April&nbsp;25, 2008 (the &#147;Underwriting Agreement&#148;), are incorporated by reference in the Terms Agreement. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">PMI has filed with
the Securities and Exchange Commission a Prospectus dated February&nbsp;21, 2014 and a Prospectus Supplement (the &#147;Prospectus Supplement&#148;) dated April&nbsp;28, 2016 (Registration No.&nbsp;333-194059) in connection with the public offering
of the Notes. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">The Notes are subject to certain customary covenants, including limitations on PMI&#146;s ability, with significant
exceptions, to incur debt secured by liens and engage in sale/leaseback transactions. PMI may redeem the 2023 Notes, in whole or in part, at the applicable redemption prices described in the Prospectus Supplement, plus accrued and unpaid interest
thereon to, but excluding, the applicable redemption date. PMI may also redeem all, but not part, of the Notes of each series upon the occurrence of specified tax events as described in the Prospectus Supplement. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">Interest on the 2023 Notes is payable from May&nbsp;10, 2016 semiannually in arrears on May&nbsp;10 and November&nbsp;10, commencing
November&nbsp;10, 2016, to holders of record on the preceding April&nbsp;26 and October&nbsp;26, as the case may be. Interest on the 2044 Notes is payable from May&nbsp;10, 2016 semiannually in arrears on May&nbsp;10 and November&nbsp;10 of each
year, commencing November&nbsp;10, 2016, to holders of record on the preceding April&nbsp;26 or October&nbsp;26, as the case may be. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The 2023 Notes will
mature on May&nbsp;10, 2023 and the 2044 Notes will mature on November&nbsp;10, 2044. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">The Notes will be PMI&#146;s senior unsecured
obligations and will rank equally in right of payment with all of its existing and future senior unsecured indebtedness. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">For a complete
description of the terms and conditions of the Underwriting Agreement, the Terms Agreement and the Notes, please refer to such agreements and the form of Notes, each of which is incorporated herein by reference and is an exhibit to this report as
Exhibits 1.1, 1.2, 4.1 and 4.2, respectively. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">Certain of the Underwriters and their respective affiliates have, from time to time,
performed, and may in the future perform, various financial advisory, commercial and investment banking services for PMI, for which they received or will receive customary fees and expenses. Certain of the Underwriters and their respective
affiliates are lenders under PMI&#146;s credit facilities. In addition, certain of the Underwriters or their respective affiliates act as dealers in connection with PMI&#146;s commercial paper programs. </P>
