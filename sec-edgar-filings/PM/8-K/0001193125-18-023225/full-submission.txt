&#147;Credit Agreement&#148;), with each lender named therein and Citibank Europe PLC, UK Branch (legal successor to Citibank International Limited), as administrative agent (the &#147;Extension Agreement&#148;). The Extension Agreement extends the

issuances. PMI and some of its subsidiaries may enter into foreign exchange and other derivative arrangements with certain of the lenders under the Credit Agreement and their respective affiliates. In addition, certain of the lenders under the
Credit Agreement and their respective affiliates act as dealers in connection with PMI&#146;s commercial paper programs. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The description above of the
