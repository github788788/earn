successor to Citibank International Limited), as administrative agent (the &#147;Extension Agreement&#148;). The Extension Agreement extends the expiration date of the Credit Agreement from February&nbsp;5, 2019 to February&nbsp;4, 2020 pursuant to
Section&nbsp;2.19 of the Credit Agreement. All other material terms and conditions of the Credit Agreement remain in full force and effect. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Some of the

receive customary fees and expenses. Certain affiliates of the lenders under the Credit Agreement are underwriters of certain of PMI&#146;s note issuances. PMI and some of its subsidiaries may enter into foreign exchange and other derivative
arrangements with certain of the lenders under the Credit Agreement and their respective affiliates. In addition, certain of the lenders under the Credit Agreement and their respective affiliates act as dealers in connection with PMI&#146;s
commercial paper programs. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The description above of the Extension Agreement is a summary and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text
