 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Item&nbsp;8.01. Other Events. </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">On May&nbsp;9, 2016, PMI issued &#128;500,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its 2.000% Notes due 2036 (the &#147;Notes&#148;). The Notes
were issued pursuant to an Indenture (the &#147;Indenture&#148;) dated as of April&nbsp;25, 2008, by and between PMI and HSBC Bank USA, National Association, as trustee (the &#147;Trustee&#148;). </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">In connection with the issuance of the Notes, on May&nbsp;3, 2016, PMI entered into a Terms Agreement (the &#147;Terms Agreement&#148;) with
the underwriters named therein (the &#147;Underwriters&#148;), pursuant to which PMI agreed to issue and sell the Notes to the Underwriters. The provisions of an Underwriting Agreement, dated as of April&nbsp;25, 2008 (the &#147;Underwriting
Agreement&#148;), are incorporated by reference in the Terms Agreement. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">PMI has filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission a
Prospectus dated February&nbsp;21, 2014 and a Prospectus Supplement (the &#147;Prospectus Supplement&#148;) dated May&nbsp;3, 2016 (Registration No.&nbsp;333-194059) in connection with the public offering of the Notes. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">The Notes are subject to certain customary covenants, including limitations on PMI&#146;s ability, with significant exceptions, to incur debt
secured by liens and engage in sale/leaseback transactions. PMI may redeem the Notes, in whole or in part, at the applicable redemption prices described in the Prospectus Supplement, plus accrued and unpaid interest thereon to, but excluding, the
applicable redemption date. PMI may also redeem all, but not part, of the Notes upon the occurrence of specified tax events as described in the Prospectus Supplement. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">Interest on the Notes is payable annually on May&nbsp;9 of each year, commencing May&nbsp;9, 2017, to holders of record on the preceding
April&nbsp;24. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Notes will mature on May&nbsp;9, 2036. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">The Notes will be PMI&#146;s senior unsecured obligations and will rank equally in right of payment with all of its existing and future senior
unsecured indebtedness. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">For a complete description of the terms and conditions of the Underwriting Agreement, the Terms Agreement and the
Notes, please refer to such agreements and the form of Notes, each of which is incorporated herein by reference and is an exhibit to this report as Exhibits 1.1, 1.2 and 4.1, respectively. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">Certain of the Underwriters and their respective affiliates have, from time to time, performed, and may in the future perform, various
financial advisory, commercial and investment banking services for PMI, for which they received or will receive customary fees and expenses. Certain of the Underwriters or their respective affiliates are lenders under PMI&#146;s credit facilities.
PMI and some of its subsidiaries may enter into foreign exchange and other derivative arrangements with certain of the Underwriters and their affiliates. In addition, certain of the Underwriters or their respective affiliates act as dealers in
connection with PMI&#146;s commercial paper programs. </P>
