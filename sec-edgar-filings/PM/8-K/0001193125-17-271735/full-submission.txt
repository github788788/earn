<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><B>Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement. </B></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On August&nbsp;29, 2017, Philip Morris International Inc.
(&#147;PMI&#148;) entered into an agreement, effective October&nbsp;1, 2017, to extend the term of its existing $3.5 billion revolving credit facility, dated as of October&nbsp;1, 2015 (as amended or modified from time to time, the &#147;Credit
Agreement&#148;), with each lender named therein, Citibank Europe PLC, UK Branch (legal successor to Citibank International Limited), as facility agent, and Citibank, N.A., as swingline agent (the &#147;Extension Agreement&#148;). The Extension
Agreement extends the expiration date of the Credit Agreement from October&nbsp;1, 2021 to October&nbsp;1, 2022 pursuant to Section&nbsp;2.23 of the Credit Agreement. All other material terms and conditions of the Credit Agreement remain in full
force and effect. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Some of the lenders under the Credit Agreement and their respective affiliates have, from time to time, performed, and may in the
future perform, various financial advisory, commercial and investment banking services for PMI, for which they received or will receive customary fees and expenses. Certain affiliates of the lenders under the Credit Agreement are underwriters of
certain of PMI&#146;s note issuances. PMI and some of its subsidiaries may enter into foreign exchange and other derivative arrangements with certain of the lenders under the Credit Agreement and their respective affiliates. In addition, certain of
the lenders under the Credit Agreement and their respective affiliates act as dealers in connection with PMI&#146;s commercial paper programs. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The
description above of the Extension Agreement is a summary and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the Extension Agreement, which is filed as Exhibit 10.1 to this report and incorporated herein by reference. The Credit
Agreement was previously filed as Exhibit 10.1 to PMI&#146;s Current Report on Form 8-K (File No.&nbsp;1-33708) filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on October&nbsp;5, 2015. </P>
