<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><b>Item 2.02 Results of Operations and Financial Condition.</b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0">Uber Technologies, Inc.&#8217;s (the &#8220;Company&#8217;s&#8221;)
Delivery business recorded approximately 32 million Uber Eats delivery trips in the UK in the three months ended September 30,
2020, compared to approximately 12.5 million trips in the three months ended September 30, 2019.</p>
